About Creovisia

Built for the classrooms of a billion learners

Creovisia is a digital product studio reimagining education for the Indian subcontinent and beyond. We believe every student deserves instant, intelligent support — and every teacher deserves tools that multiply their impact.

Founded in Tamil Nadu, we are a lean, ambitious team building AI-native SaaS platforms purpose-built for the realities of Indian education: multiple languages, state boards, and the relentless demand for quality.

Our parent brand houses a growing ecosystem of products — each one precision-crafted to solve a real problem at the intersection of learning and technology.
