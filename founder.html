Founder of Creovisia

Hello! I’m Mukil Karki M, a passionate student, tech enthusiast, and the founder of Creovisia. I started Creovisia with a simple mission — to make high-quality tech education accessible, practical, and free for everyone.

From building websites and designing interfaces to learning cybersecurity and creating content, I believe real skills come from real projects.

Creovisia is not just a platform — it’s a growing community of learners, creators, and builders who want to turn ideas into impact.

My long-term vision is to build Creovisia into a complete digital ecosystem for students: learning, collaboration, tools, and opportunities — all in one place.
